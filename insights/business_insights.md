# 📊 E-commerce Sales Insights

## 🔹 Key Findings

- The majority of revenue is generated from a few product categories such as Set and Kurta
- Sales are highly concentrated in specific states like Maharashtra and Karnataka
- A small number of categories drive most of the business performance
- Revenue distribution is uneven across regions

## 🔹 Business Recommendations

- Focus on top-performing categories to maximize revenue
- Expand marketing efforts in high-performing states
- Improve performance in low-performing regions
- Optimize inventory for high-demand products

## 🔹 Conclusion

The analysis highlights key revenue drivers and provides actionable insights to improve sales performance and business strategy.
